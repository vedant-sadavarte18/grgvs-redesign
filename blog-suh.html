Safe Haven and Hope: Ahmedabad’s Shelters for Urban Homeless (SUH) – GRGVS with AMC/UCD !

Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti (GRGVS) is proud to inform that we’re managing the Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation’s (AMC) Shelters for Urban Homeless (SUH), in close collaboration with the Urban Community Development Department (UCD) and supported by the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM)!

Our mission is to provide safe haven, essential support services, and a path towards a better life for Ahmedabad’s homeless population.

We currently operate shelters at two locations at Ahmedabad:
1) IIM Ahmedabad Shelter: Near Andh Jan Mandal, Opposite IIM Ahmedabad New Campus, Under IIM Flyover (Capacity: 60 Persons)
2) Jhansi Ni Rani Shelter: Near Bimanagar Garden (Capacity: 48 Persons)

SUH Services:
a. 24/7 Safe Accommodation
b. Nutritious Meals (for elderly, weak, mentally challenged and children)
c. Well-equipped facilities
d. Childcare services linked with Anganwadi Centers/Schools/Education
e. Connection to social welfare schemes and livelihood opportunities

We welcome you to visit our shelter homes and learn more about Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH). We require CSR / FCRA support to make these shelters self-sustainable, enhance amenities, renovation, skill development, medical camps, and education programmes @ these shelter homes.

A. Support our mission of empowering the homeless
B. Volunteer your time and skills to make a difference.
C. Please raise awareness about availability of these shelter homes so that maximum underprivileged people can take benefit.
D. Corporates based in Ahmedabad are encouraged to visit these shelters and advise us on making them model shelter homes in the city.

Shelter Homes CSR initiatives are in line with Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013 and related to several UNSDGs, such as : Goal 1: No Poverty, Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being, Goal 4: Quality Education, Goal 5: Gender Equality, Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth, Goal 10: Reduced Inequality, Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities, Goal 16: Partnerships for the Goals, Goal 17: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions
