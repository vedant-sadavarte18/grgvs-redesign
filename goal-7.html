Goal 7: Education Support

School Project: Building a Better Future

Infrastructure Development for Government and Granted Schools in Gujarat

Introduction: In Gujarat, many government and granted schools lack basic infrastructure and facilities, including sanitation, libraries, additional rooms, sheds, and RO plants etc. To bridge this gap and provide better educational opportunities, we have initiated a CSR project to develop infrastructure in government and granted schools.

Project Description: Develop infrastructure in government and granted schools across Gujarat. The project will cover several aspects, including sanitation facilities, library development, additional rooms, sheds, and RO plants.

Impact & Benefits:

  • It will help to improve the learning environment of students and promote a culture of cleanliness and hygiene.
  • The availability of clean and safe drinking water will also have a positive impact on the health of students and staff.
  • The development of infrastructure will create a more conducive learning environment and help to bridge the gap between the quality of education provided in government and private schools.

Benefits to the Corporate Partner:

  • Branding Board, recognition
  • Employee Engagement Opportunities
  • Support Local Communities: Direct impact on the lives of local communities and students, fostering inclusive growth and development

Alignment with SDGs: 4, 8, 10, 5, 17

Education Project: Shaping Bright Futures

Psychometric Test and Career Counseling Seminars for Underprivileged Students in Gujarat

Introduction: Providing psychometric testing and career counseling to underprivileged students in Gujarat.

Project Description: The Psychometric Test and Career Counseling Seminars initiative aims to identify and support underprivileged students in classes 9, 10, 11, and 12 in government and granted schools in Gujarat. It includes psychometric testing, group counseling, and follow-up sessions, with a focus on building confidence, promoting education and career opportunities, and challenging gender stereotypes.

Impact & Benefits:

  • By providing psychometric testing and career counseling, the project aims to empower students and help them make informed decisions about their future.

Benefits to the Corporate Partner:

  • Branding Board, recognition
  • Employee Engagement Opportunities
  • Support deserving students and contribute towards social development.

Alignment with SDGs: 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 11, 17
