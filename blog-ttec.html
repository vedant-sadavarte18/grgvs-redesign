On behalf of our NGO, I’m thrilled to invite you to join the 22nd Annual TTEC Wellness Walk in Ahmedabad on Sunday, February 18th, 2024. This event promises a fun and meaningful way to walk for a cause, connect with colleagues, and make a positive impact.
