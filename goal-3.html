Goal 3: Rural Development

Integrated Rural Development Project under CSR

Introduction: The Integrated Rural Development Project is an initiative aimed at improving the living conditions of people living in rural areas of Gujarat. The project is being implemented by the Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti (GRGVS) with the support of corporate companies through their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives.

Project Description: The project will be implemented in villages of Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Kheda, Botad, and other districts. It will adopt a holistic approach to rural development, focusing on improving multiple aspects of rural life. The project will include the construction of Anganwadi centers to provide early childhood education, nutrition, and healthcare services to children and pregnant women. The project will also involve the construction and improvement of roads, bridges, and drainage systems to enhance connectivity and promote economic development. Solar street lights will be installed to provide better lighting and ensure the safety of villagers. Tree plantation will be carried out to promote environmental conservation and enhance the beauty of the village.

The project will also focus on drinking rainwater recharge to improve access to safe and clean drinking water. Sanitation facilities will be upgraded to ensure proper waste disposal and promote hygiene in the village.

Impact: The Integrated Rural Development Project will have a significant impact on the lives of people in rural areas of Gujarat. It will provide better access to healthcare, education, and economic opportunities, especially for women and children. The project will promote environmental conservation and lead to sustainable development in the region.

Conclusion: We invite corporate companies to come forward and adopt a village in Gujarat to contribute to the development of rural areas through this project. By working together, we can create a better future for rural communities in Gujarat. The Integrated Rural Development Project provides an excellent opportunity for companies to fulfill their CSR obligations while making a significant impact on the lives of people in rural areas.

Shelter Home Project - Shelters for Urban Homeless (SUH)

Introduction: Supporting and Strengthening Shelters for Urban Homeless (SUH) in Ahmedabad. Management and operation of Shelters for Urban Homeless (SUH) in Ahmedabad. Partnering with the Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation. Managed in close collaboration with UCD under DAY-NULM mission.

GRGVS manages two strategic urban shelters:

  • Jhansi Ni Rani: Near Bimanagar Garden, with a capacity for 48 individuals.
  • IIM Ahmedabad: Accommodating 60 individuals.

Project Description: We aim to enhance the facilities at our shelters through this CSR initiative and seek the support of corporate partners to supply specific items or provide funding.

Impact on the Homeless Community:

  • Restoring Dignity and Well-being: Transform the lives of Urban Homeless by enhancing their self-esteem, increasing personal self-sufficiency, and improving overall well-being.
  • Digital Support and Skilling: Integration of computers provides digital support & access to online government schemes.
  • Recreational Facilities: Installation of swings & communal areas provides essential recreational spaces.

CSR Opportunities for Collaboration:

  • Infrastructure & Sustainability Upgrades: Support solar systems, RO water plants, & rainwater harvesting to reduce operational costs.
  • Health and Nutrition Programs: Sponsor nutrition programs (food/supplements) & organize health/medical camps.
  • Education and Skill Development: Engage corporate volunteers in career counseling, digital literacy training, & skill development workshops.
  • Corporate Visibility & Recognition: Receive branding on-site, recognition in project reports, & direct employee engagement opportunities.
