Volunteer Opportunities and Internship Programs

At Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti, we offer a range of volunteer opportunities and internship programs for companies and college students who want to get involved with our organization and help us make a positive impact on the communities we serve.

Volunteer Opportunities:

We welcome volunteers from all walks of life. Some of the ways you can contribute include:

  • Teaching underprivileged children & promoting digital literacy.
  • Organizing and participating in community events.
  • Managing social media and creating engaging content.
  • Fundraising, grant writing, and sponsor outreach.
  • Developing visual content through photography.
  • Participating in ongoing rural development projects.

Upon completion, we provide a certificate of participation.

Internship Program:

Designed to offer college students a hands-on learning experience in the development sector, fulfilling their college course requirements and project works. You'll work alongside experienced staff, contribute to meaningful projects, and build your resume. A certificate of participation is provided upon successful completion.
