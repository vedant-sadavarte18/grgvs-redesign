Volunteer & Internship

Volunteer Opportunities

At Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti, we offer a range of volunteer opportunities for individuals who want to get involved with our organization and help us make a positive impact on the communities we serve.

We welcome volunteers from all walks of life. Some of the ways you can contribute include:

  • Teaching underprivileged children & promoting digital literacy.
  • Organizing and participating in community events.
  • Managing social media and creating engaging content.
  • Fundraising, grant writing, and sponsor outreach.
  • Developing visual content through photography.
  • Participating in ongoing rural development projects.

Upon completion, we provide a certificate of participation.

Internship Programs

Designed to offer college students a hands-on learning experience in the development sector, fulfilling their college course requirements and project works. You'll work alongside experienced staff, contribute to meaningful projects, and build your resume. We offer diverse roles in program management, community outreach, data analysis, and communications. Our structured programs ensure you gain practical skills while making a real-world impact.

What You Will Gain:

  • Hands-on Experience: Apply classroom theories to real-world challenges in rural development and education.
  • Mentorship: Receive guidance and feedback from seasoned professionals in the NGO sector.
  • Skill Development: Enhance your leadership, communication, and project management capabilities.
  • Professional Networking: Connect with community leaders, stakeholders, and like-minded peers.

A certificate of participation and a letter of recommendation are provided upon successful completion.
