Support Our Cause

Since 1988, Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti has worked tirelessly to uplift rural communities across Gujarat. We believe that every person deserves access to basic needs and a better life.

Our Focus

Creating sustainable impact through education, health, environmental protection, rural development, and women's empowerment.

Your Impact
