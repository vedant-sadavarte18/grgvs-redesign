Goal 2: Anganwadi : ICDS

Anganwadi : Building Strong Foundations

Transforming Anganwadis with Modern Infrastructure in association with ICDS : Various CSR Initiatives

  • Project for New Anganwadi Centre
  • Project for Renovation and Repairing of Existing Anganwadi Centre
  • Project for SMART Anganwadi Centre
  • Project for Provision of Toilet Facility at Anganwadi Centre
  • Project for Providing RO Plant, Toys, Charts, Infantometer, Stove, Kitchen Equipments as per ICDS Requirements
  • Capacity Building of Anganwadi Centre : Seminars, Training and Visit of Volunteers

Gujarat Rajya Gram Vikas Samiti has successfully completed 50 projects for the construction of Anganwadi centers in various villages of Gujarat. Our exceptional expertise, timely delivery, and outstanding designs have been appreciated by all stakeholders, including District Administrations, ICDS, NITI Aayog Representatives, and CSR Companies.

We take pride in building Anganwadi centers that are not only beautiful but also functional and safe. Our focus on creating a nurturing and welcoming environment for young children has earned us accolades and recognition from across the region. Our team has also renovated and repaired existing Anganwadi centers, transforming them into model and smart centers that meet the needs of modern-day children and communities.

We extend a warm invitation to companies to join hands with us in implementing this project in Kheda, Gandhinagar, Ahmedabad, Botad, Dahod, and other districts of Gujarat. By partnering with us, your company can contribute to the development of society while fulfilling its CSR obligations.

We believe that every child deserves a safe and nurturing environment to learn and grow, and we are committed to providing the same through our Anganwadi projects. Our team's dedication and hard work have made a difference in the lives of thousands of children in Gujarat, and we are committed to continuing this work with the support of like-minded individuals and organizations. Join us today, and together we can make a difference in the lives of children in rural Gujarat.

Impact & Benefits

The project is expected to have a significant positive impact on the lives of the beneficiaries in the Villages of Districts of Gujarat. The project will improve the quality of early childhood education and care, ensure the safety, health, and well-being of children, promote gender equality.

Alignment with SDGs

SDG: 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 17

Benefits to the Corporate Partner

  • Branding Board, recognition
  • Company will be mentioned in all project-related materials such as progress reports
  • Employee Engagement Opportunities
  • Company will be able to build relationships with Community and Government
